Design

Concept

I wanted to create a more grown-up version of the cover for the book, featuring a cleaner style with a little more gore. I decided to include things from the book itself such as the footprints from the Marauder's Map and Padfoots paw print painted in fake blood, a silver feather for Buckbeak and Hermione's Time-Turner. After laying out and painting the design on paper and photographing it, I edited the photo digitally and added the text.

Font

I decided to forgo the traditional Harry Potter font and went for a classic British font for a classic printed look.
