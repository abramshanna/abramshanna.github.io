Design

In the first phase of the project the design was established and assets were created for implementation.

Colors

The colors used in the project were inspired by Ed Sheerans branding and album covers, featuring a limited range of colors.

Fonts

I paired three different fonts to make the design more dynamic and playful. The main font gave it a handdrawn feel while the other bolder fonts were used for emphasis.

Illustrations

The illustrations were inspired by the lyrics and I created individual pieces for different parts of the song, keeping the style minimalistic.

Illustration of tattoos based on Ed Sheeran motives.

Implementation

Once the design style was established the assets were implemented and the video was animated.

Don't miss a beat

Each part of the video had to be animated by hand as to match the tempo of the song as it did not follow a specific pattern and some assets could not be completely reused. To make it visually interesting I played with composition and colors, fonts and illustrations and animating text to appear in various ways depending on the music.

Final Product

Once the implementation was completed the final product resulted in a lyric video featuring the entire song.
